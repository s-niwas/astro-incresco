We at Incresco, are committed to protecting and preserving your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, Incresco (hereby to be mentioned as platform) and the services associated with the platform. Our privacy policy governs and regulates our data collection, processing and usage practices. If you do not agree with the data practices described in this privacy policy, you should not use the application or the services.

### Information that we gather

We collect it by fair and lawful means, such as registrations from our website, directly from you when you enquire or sign up for an account. Our website permits you to contact us via email or mobile number or message box. Whenever you send an email via our site, you will be providing us with your name, title, email address, institution name and phone number, and any information you choose to include in the text of your message.

### How we use the information

We collect and use personal information to allow us to provide our products and services to our clients and end users. We use the data to to communicate information and to apply it to our marketing and engagement activities such as contacting the relevant staff or clients by email, mail, or by telephone.

### About data security

Your privacy is important to us. Our policy is to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, https://increscotech.com/, and other sites or platforms we own and operate.

We may display content that links to third-party websites. We cannot control or be held responsible for third parties’ privacy practices and content. If you click on a third-party advertisement or link, please understand that you are leaving the Incresco website and any personal data you provide will not be covered by this Policy. Please read their privacy policies to find out how they collect and process your personal data.

### Privacy policy updation

Incresco may periodically update this policy. We encourage you to periodically review our website for the latest information on our privacy practices.
